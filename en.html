SUPPORT & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI-driven workforce management solution.

How does the AI matching work? expand_more
Our advanced AI tools analyze the most critical data points — including skills, availability, location, and past performance — to quickly match qualified professionals with your shift requirements.
What industries do you serve? expand_more
We currently specialize in Light Industrial, Warehousing, Logistics, Hospitality, and Event Staffing — and we also support other sectors with similar skill demands and shift-based workforce needs.
stars Does aplyk employ and pay the workers? expand_more

No.

aplyk is a technology platform that connects businesses with vetted professionals for temporary shifts. We do not act as the Employer of Record and we do not run payroll. The hiring company remains responsible for contracts, payroll, and statutory obligations, while aplyk provides the tools to manage the workflow — matching, shift confirmations, time tracking, and performance feedback.

How quickly can I get workers? expand_more
Our on-demand network is active 24/7. For most metro areas, we can fill shift requests in as little as 2 hours. However, posting shifts 24-48 hours in advance guarantees the highest quality matches.
Is compliance handled automatically? expand_more
Yes. Our platform automatically enforces break times, overtime rules, and local labor laws based on the worker's location, reducing your liability and ensuring full legal compliance effortlessly.
